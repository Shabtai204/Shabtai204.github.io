About BlackEagle

Create your account and control up to 5 devices securely, and for free. You may choose which sites you would like to block or choose a pre-made list of sites by category. E.g. Social networks, Adults content, Gambling sites and more.. The service also allow you to fully control a remote computer and send requests for different types of data. Lock, take screenshots, activate camera or microphone, collect keylogs and browser history and much more.

  • - TLS 1.2
  • - AES-256 encryption
  • - Two factor authentication by default
  • - Secure password policy
  • - SHA256 and salt for secure storage of login data
  • - Brute force prevention
  • - Denial of service prevention
  • - Role based authorization
  • - Secure against SQL-injections
  • - XSS attack prevention
